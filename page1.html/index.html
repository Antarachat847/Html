    I’m a spirited Indian girl blending the vibrant colors of my roots with the dynamic energy of New York City. At Parsons School of Design, I draw inspiration from Indian textiles and contemporary design, weaving a story of cultural fusion and artistic expression. Here, I’m chasing dreams and painting my journey, one bold stroke at a time.

I had the privilege of collaborating with Cadbury on their Diwali packaging for their e-commerce platform, a creatively fulfilling and inspiring experience.

The project celebrated togetherness, a cornerstone of Diwali, through a unique card game designed to foster family connections as well as custom box packaging which included a picture frame, goodie bag filled with choclates as well as a personalised message making this the perfect gift to celebrate this festival of lights and unity.
